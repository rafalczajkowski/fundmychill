What's this about?

I graduated university with a degree I thought I wanted, worked a few jobs, got a postgrad degree, and earned a few certifications. But I never really felt truly interested in these roles, and the perspective of working for the rest of my life just makes me ughhh.

I'm not lazy (okay, maybe a little), but I value freedom and flexibility over traditional success. I'd rather perfect tea brewing techniques, read random books, and maybe try to do creative stuff than climb any corporate ladder. I'm not really materialistic - you won't catch me buying too expensive stuff. Although I would like a Volkswagen T1 or a small plane... (first I'd need a pilot's license, though)

Right now, I work in social media. In my free time, I stare at the garden, experiment with tea blends, play retro games, and attempt to learn languages with the attention span of a goldfish. I also built this website, which was more fun than anything I've done at my day job in months.

Now I'm creating digital products - mainly ebooks and other cozy resources - for people who think life should be more about enjoying your morning tea than optimizing your LinkedIn profile.
